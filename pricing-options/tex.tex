\(\Delta = e^{-\delta h} \frac{C_u - C_d}{S_u - S_d} \) \\
\( B = e^{-rh}(C_u - e^{\delta h} \Delta S_u) \)\\

Let \( v \) be log-return over one time step \( h \).\\

\vspace{-1em}
\[
v^2 = 0.25 (\ln u - \ln d)^2
\Rightarrow
u = d e^{2 \sigma\sqrt{h}} 
\]
\vspace{-2em}
\[
